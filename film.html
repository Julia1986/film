Frozen 2 rakes in $350 million worldwide on box office debut

Frozen 2 raked in $350 million (nearly £272m) in its opening weekend worldwide, beating forecasts and the box office debut of the original film. The sequel made about £15m in the UK and Ireland and $127m (£98.9m) in the US and Canada, which are not counted towards the worldwide figures. The 2013 original took $93m (£72.28m) during its first five days in theatres, according to Reuters.

The Year Of Keanu Reeves May Not Happen In 2021 Anymore

Keanu Reeves was supposed to have a banner year in 2021 with the release of The Matrix 4 and John Wick: Chapter 4, but that seems unlikely to happen now. Back in 2014, Reeves experienced a renaissance when the first John Wick movie came out, reaffirming his status as one of this generation's greatest action stars. Becoming a sleeper hit, John Wick spawned a franchise and two sequels were released in 2017 and 2019. Both followups were successful critically and commercially, paving the way for a fourth film.
